Ever since I was in senior high school, software development has been my passion. I still remember sitting in front of Pentium III computer in my bed room writing computer program that printed "Hello, World!" in PASCAL for the very first time. As I went through the tutorial, it was almost a magic, being able to get computer to do whatever I wanted, started with 'begin' and ended with 'end' syntax and limited only by my own imagination.

Now in industry, this passion remains with me. There's certainly yet more to learn, yet more problem to solve, and yet more to build. And for this, I am very grateful.

Hi, I am Luthfi, an experienced Software Engineer with strong IT background. I have been through a lot of experiences in working as individual or with team. Solving problem with best and proper method is what I do. And I believe the world will be more beautiful place through quality software.
